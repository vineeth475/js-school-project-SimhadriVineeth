Summary Table of Events

Year Key Events
1939 Germany invades Poland. Britain and France declare war on Germany.
1940 France falls. Battle of Britain begins.
1941 Germany invades USSR. Japan attacks Pearl Harbor.
1942 Turning points: Midway and Stalingrad.
1943 Italy surrenders. Allies advance in Europe.
1944 D-Day landings in Normandy. Paris liberated.
1945 Germany surrenders. Atomic bombs dropped on Hiroshima and Nagasaki. Japan surrenders.
